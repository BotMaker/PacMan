 PacMan
 
 This is a kid game like PacMan.
 But you cannot eat the ghosts here.
 You have to open treasure chests.
 To open a treasure chest you need a key.
 Collect up all gold and coins.
 If you have done everything, then run to the escape door
 Use the cursor keys for the movement of your hero.
 Use the space bar to drop a bomb. 
 You can play 37 levels.
 
 Menu:  
 use the arrow keys up/down/return.
